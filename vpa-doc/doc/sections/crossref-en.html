IRI: http://data.europa.eu/949/vpa/AppropriateBody

Accredited organization which is able to express a formal opinion on Requirements, expressing them in DocumentedEvidence (mostly called Certificates), and if necessary making them dependent on Restrictions, which shall be fulfilled.

Permitting Bodyc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://data.europa.eu/949/vpa/PermittingBody

Within its legally recognized mandate, this Body is able to receive DocumentedEvidence as submitted in a Request and based upon it, issue a Permit to the RequestingBody for the activities covered by the mentioned legislation.
has super-classes
Body c

IRI: http://data.europa.eu/949/vpa/Request

Requesting Bodyc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://data.europa.eu/949/vpa/RequestingBody

Within its legally recognized role, this Body is able to express the intention to act under the condition of reception of a Permission. The intended action being covered by legal or standardized Requirements to fullfil, this Body will present all required information, as verified by the Appropriate and examined by the Permitting Bodies, to the latter in order to receive the mentioned Permission.
has super-classes
Body c
is in domain of
issues op

Requirementc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://data.europa.eu/949/vpa/Requirement

A legal/standardised collection of rules, which must be verified independently to be met in the context of a Permission Request, and of which the measure of compliance is expressed in DocumentedEvidence, with or without added Restrictions.
is in range of
certifies op, closes op, regarding op

Restrictionc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://data.europa.eu/949/vpa/Restriction

Issued by an Appropriate Body in connection with its documented evidence on a certain requirement, it contains all aspects to take into account in order to accept the DocumentedEvidence as proof, enabling the scope of the Permission. It is possible that the Restriction limits the Permission in the same way.
is in domain of
regarding op
is in range of
with restriction op
